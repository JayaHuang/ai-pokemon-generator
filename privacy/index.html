Privacy Policy

At AI Pokemon Generator, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free AI Pokemon character generation tool.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our tool. We do not store your descriptions, generated characters, or any other content you create using our service.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our service. Google Analytics collects anonymous data such as page views, session duration, and geographic location. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Third-Party Services

Our tool uses Pollinations AI for image generation. When you generate a character, your description is sent to Pollinations AI servers. Please refer to Pollinations AI privacy policy for information on how they handle this data.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for analytics purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
